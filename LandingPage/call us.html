Some tips when you talk to Keya, this will help her in understanding your query

  • Always keep your banking details handy as you may need it for validation while you talk to Keya
  • Speak as you would normally but clearly. After all, Keya is eager to help you
  • Try and ensure there is no background noise so she can hear your instructions in one go
  • Help her out by rephrasing your question or try to make it more specific. Use short sentences instead of paragraphs. For example, there are many banking requests called "Statement". If you want to get the bank account statement, say, "Need my bank account statement"
  • In case you need to speak our customer care executive, try saying, "Want to speak to an officer"
